COVID-19 Semester Grade Calculator

The grading policy has been decided for this unusual semester. Your effort in both quarters matter, but the more successful quarter will have more impact. Your semester grade will be calculated:

  • 40% from your Q3 grade
  • 40% from your Q4 grade
  • 20% will come from the larger of either Q3 or Q4

The reason for this choice is two-fold: 1) to help shelter anyone that has a terrible experience during the stay-at-home order and 2) celebrate anyone who finds that they thrive when working from home, more than in the traditional classroom.
